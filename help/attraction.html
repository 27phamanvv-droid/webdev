Legendary JDM Cars

Daikoku is famous for rare Japanese classics like the Toyota Supra MK4, Nissan Skyline GT-R, and RX-7 builds pushing insane horsepower numbers.

Exotic Supercars

You’ll find Lamborghinis, Ferraris, and McLarens lining up side-by-side in exclusive reserved spots during night meets.

Midnight Car Meets

The real magic happens after midnight — engines roaring, neon lights glowing, and enthusiasts comparing builds and performance stats.
