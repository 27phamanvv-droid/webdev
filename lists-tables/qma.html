Color Scheme Explanation

I chose an analogous color scheme using blue and green tones because these colors are commonly associated with calmness, trust, and professionalism. Blue helps convey reliability and focus, while green adds a sense of balance and growth. This combination creates a clean, modern look that is easy on the eyes and well-suited for a technology-focused webpage.
